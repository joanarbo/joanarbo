Principios y Visión

Staff+ Engineering Philosophy

Frameworks for technical leadership and decision-making at scale

Technical Vision

Setting multi-year technical direction while balancing innovation with pragmatism. Focus on systems that scale beyond the next quarter.

  • Think in systems, not features
  • Optimize for change, not perfection
  • Build leverage through abstraction

Multiplier Mindset

Impact through enabling others. The best Staff+ engineers make their entire organization more effective.

  • Document decisions, not just code
  • Mentor through code reviews
  • Create reusable patterns

Decision Framework

Structured approach to technical decisions that balances speed, quality, and long-term maintainability.

  • Reversible vs. irreversible decisions
  • Cost of delay vs. cost of change
  • Local optimization vs. global impact
